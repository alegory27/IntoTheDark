Ignore the Light

Doubtful of the light, you believe its great promise is nothing more than a great lie.

Yeeeaaaaah.... I'm pretty sure the last place I want to be is there. Who knows what's over there just waiting to pounce on the unfortunate soul to have hope in this dreadful place. Noooo thank you!

You keep onwards towards the dark unknown. Feeling there is safety in not being seen. And that is where you will remain for the rest of your days. Forever wandering the dark, fearful of the light.

The End
