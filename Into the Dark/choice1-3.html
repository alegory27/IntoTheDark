Remain Silent

You cease all sound. Straining your ears as hard as you can. Listening to the groaning sounds steadily come closer.

Dedicated to your cause, you remain motionless and silent. By some miracle the sound seems to be going away!

Whatever is out there doesn't sound too friendly, and it appears it can't see me in this dreadful darkness any more than I can see it! Still.... I'd like to put as much distance as possilbe between us...
