Flee

Fear consumes your being. Calling out to you as loudly as the mysterious groans.

As you start to panic, the darkness around grows. You finally start moving your legs and carry yourself away into who knows what. Your racing thoughts only adding more to your confusion.

I must keep moving, I must keep moving. I can't see a damn thing! Where the hell am I!? What was that frightening sound?!

You manage to work yourself into a full out sprint, and after what seems to be a few minutes you trip on something hard laying on the ground. You go crashing into what appears to be a tree and smack your head on it's trunk.
