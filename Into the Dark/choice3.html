Stillness

Confusion and panic begin to creep into your mind. The silence is unnerving. It almost seems sentient. Wanting to take hold of it's new prey.

You're determined to remove it's influence from your mind. After all, it does one no good to panic when lost. Especially one so far from home.

You position yourself upright. Trying to get as comfortable as you can on the hard ground. You close your eyes and turn your focus inward. Concentrating on your breath.

Time starts to lose meaning as you feel the tension slowly withdraw from your awareness. You open your eyes and know what you must do.

I need to start moving. I need to start looking for a way out of here.

You stand up and prepare yourself for your journey.
