The Box

You sit back against the tree and bring the box up close to you. Without further delay, you pry open the lid.

Inside you find a note.

The Note Reads...

6 9 4 2
9 4 2 6
4 2 6 9
? ? ? ?

You scoff and roll your eyes.

Oh, lovely, this is very helpful...

You study the note very carefully. Perhaps the solution will be of use later.
